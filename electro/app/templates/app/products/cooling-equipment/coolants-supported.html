Coolants Supported

ELECTRO IMPULSE LAB., INC. Supports a Wide Variety of Coolants

ELECTRO IMPULSE LAB., INC.'s Cooling Equipment supports a wide variety of standard and specialty coolants. The characteristics of coolants differ so significantly that they can dictate the design of the cooling system, so it is important to select the proper coolant. See the Selecting Coolants and Coolant Section Chart pages for more details on Coolant parameters. Listed below are some of the coolants available with ELECTRO IMPULSE LAB., INC.'s Cooling Equipment.

  • MIL-PRF-87252 PAO (Poly-alphaolefin) - OUR SPECIALTY

  • Air

  • Water

  • Deionized (DI) Water

  • Ulta-pure Deionized (DI) Water

  • Ethylene Glycol & Water Solutions

  • Propylene Glycol & Water Solutions

  • Coolanol 20R, 25R, 35R

  • DC-200

  • Dowtherm J

  • Dowtherm G

  • Diala AX / Univolt 33

  • Marcol 87 (CS)

  • MIL-H-5606

  • Fluorinert - FC75/FC77/FC104/FC770

  • Dow Syltherm Silicone Oils
