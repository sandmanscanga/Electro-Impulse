RC Series Refrigerated Air Circulating Systems

ELECTRO IMPULSE LAB., INC. RC Series Refrigerated Air Circulating Systems provide air at a controlled flow rate and temperature with monitoring of pressure and temperatures. These units can be rack mounted or standalone and can have one or multiple controlled air outputs. Refrigeration and air units can be separate or combined in one unit.

Easy installation. Connect air supply lines to heat load, connect line power, ground and safety interlock.
